Services

KC Bodywork combines relaxation with therapeutic massage to achieve the most beneficial results in the muscular system as well as the nervous system. Repetitive actions in our daily lives can leave us with overactive nerves and chronic muscle tension. The goal is to address chronic and/or acute pain while achieving neuromuscular relaxation through compression, friction, joint mobilization, and stretching of the muscles. This approach allows muscle fibers to release more deeply and blood and lymph to flow through the body more freely. This will leave the client feeling more relaxed and aligned than before they walked in the door. Myofascial Massage & Bodywork helps to alleviate muscular discomfort for a healthy body and mind.

Benefits of Myofascial Massage Therapy &Bodywork

  • Alleviate compressed or irritated nerves
  • Improved & balanced structural alignment
  • Release of tendon, ligament, and bone tightness
  • Neuromuscular relaxation
  • Increased range of motion


Basalt Stones are used throughout the duration of the massage incorporating heat and the compression of the muscles.

Benefits of Hot Stone Massage

  • Heat increases blood flow
  • Reduces pain
  • Increases collagen extensibility
  • Increases range of motion
  • Decreases joint stiffness
